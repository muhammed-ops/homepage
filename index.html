ABOUT STOKE-ON-TRENT

Stoke-on-Trent (often abbreviated to Stoke)
is a city and unitary authority area in Staffordshire,
England, with an area of 36 square miles (93 km2).
In 2019, the city had an estimated population
of 256,375.[9] It is the largest settlement in Staffordshire
and is surrounded by the towns of Newcastle-under-Lyme,
Alsager, Kidsgrove, Biddulph and Stone, which form a
